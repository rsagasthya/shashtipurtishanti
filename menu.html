Lunch Menu

Served from 1 pm

Appetizers

  • Vegetable Bhaat

    A flavorful and aromatic rice dish made by cooking rice with a medley of vegetables and a blend of spices. The vegetables, such as carrots, peas, beans, and potatoes, are sautéed with mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric, and other spices to create a fragrant, savory base. The rice is then mixed in, absorbing the rich flavors.

Sides

  • Beans and Bhendi Bhaaji

    Fresh french beans sautéed with some green chillies, curry leaves and mustard seeds, and fresh okra shallow fried with a good crisp.

  • Baby corn and Guava Koshambiri

    A refreshing South Indian salad made with soaked moong dal, finely chopped cucumber, grated coconut, and a tempering of mustard seeds and curry leaves, lightly seasoned with green chilies and fresh coriander, offering a healthy, crunchy, and vibrant side dish.

  • Orange Gojju

    A sweet and tangy South Indian curry made with orange peel simmered in a spicy, tamarind-based gravy with ground coconut, mustard seeds, and aromatic spices, creating a unique blend of flavors that’s both refreshing and comforting.

  • Papad

    A thin, crispy wafer made from rice flour, seasoned with spices and sun-dried before being fried or roasted to golden perfection; a crunchy accompaniment to any Indian meal.

  • Bonda

    spiced potatoes encased in a crispy, golden batter. Deep-fried to perfection, these bite-sized treats are crunchy on the outside and soft on the inside, offering a delightful blend of flavors.

  • Khara Boondi

    A savory Indian snack made from tiny, crispy fried chickpea flour droplets, seasoned with a blend of spices like cumin, chili powder, and asafoetida.

  • Pickle

    Spicy Indian pickles made with various vegetables or mango.

Main Courses

  • Nuchinude Kadhi

    A smooth and mildly spiced dahi-based curry with tender white pumpkin pieces, tempered with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and a dash of cumin, blending tangy and sweet flavors for a comforting, wholesome dish.
    Served with rice.

  • Avarekalu Sambar

    A hearty lentil stew simmered with Avarekai, mixed vegetables and a special blend of spices, enriched with tamarind for tanginess and jaggery for a hint of sweetness, then tempered with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and dried red chilies for a deep, flavorful finish.
    Served with rice.

  • Rasam

    A tangy, spiced South Indian soup made with tamarind, tomatoes, and a blend of ground spices, tempered with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and black pepper for warmth, offering a refreshing and aromatic addition to any meal.
    Served with rice.

  • Dahi

    A creamy, cooling Indian yogurt, naturally fermented and mildly tangy.
    Served with rice.

Desserts

  • Paradi Kheer

    A traditional Indian dessert made from rice simmered in rich, sweetened milk, flavored with aromatic cardamom and saffron. This creamy pudding is garnished with crunchy dry fruits such as cashews and almonds, and finished with a touch of ghee for an indulgent experience. It is the perfect sweet ending to your meal, offering a luxurious blend of flavors and textures.

  • Pheni

    A delicate, melt-in-your-mouth dessert made from thin, crisp layers of dough, fried to a golden perfection. Light and flaky, Pheni is typically served with a drizzle of powdered sugar and warm milk, offering a perfect balance of sweetness and crunch.

  • Barfi

    A rich, fudge-like Indian sweet made from condensed milk, sugar, and ghee. It has a smooth, melt-in-your-mouth texture, flavored with cardamom and often garnished with nuts like cashews or almonds.
